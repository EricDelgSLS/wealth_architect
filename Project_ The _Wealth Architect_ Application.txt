Project: The "Wealth Architect" Application
Date: December 13, 2025 Role: Product Manager & Lead Developer Goal: Transform a text-based Investment Prompt Chain into a fully interactive Web Application.
Part 1: Product Strategy Guide (The "PM" View)
Objective: Understand the user journey, the problem we are solving, and the "why" behind the technology.
1. The Core Problem
High-earning Millennials have surplus cash but lack a cohesive strategy. They suffer from:
* Analysis Paralysis: Too many options (Crypto? ETFs? Real Estate?).
* Asset Clutter: Money scattered across old 401ks, random Robinhood stocks, and HYSA.
* Lack of Structure: No clear distinction between "Lump Sum" (clean up) and "Recurring" (build wealth).
2. The Solution: "The Wealth Architect"
An interactive web application that acts as a fiduciary-style consultant. It performs two distinct calculations:
1. The Consolidation (Lump Sum): Audits current messy assets to create a single, clean starting pile.
2. The Engine (Recurring): Calculates a sustainable biweekly contribution from salary surplus.
3. The User Flow (The Experience)
We are moving from a "Chat" experience to a "Wizard" experience.
Phase 1: The Audit & Consolidation (Finding the Lump Sum)
* Goal: Help the user identify money they already have that isn't working hard enough.
* User Action:
   * Input: "Cash in Savings"
   * Input (Optional): "Current Random Assets" (User pastes a list: 'I have $5k in AMC, $10k in an old 401k, and $2k in Bitcoin').
* System Logic (The Auditor):
   * The AI analyzes these inputs.
   * Advice: It suggests what to keep, what to sell (e.g., "Consider selling the meme stocks to fund your core portfolio"), and consolidates this into a single number: The Initial Lump Sum.
Phase 2: The Sustainability Check (Finding the Recurring Amount)
* Goal: Calculate a safe monthly contribution.
* User Action: Input "Take Home Pay" and "Fixed Monthly Bills."
* System Logic (The Calculator):
   * Calculates Surplus = Pay - Bills.
   * The Soft Guardrail: If they list high-interest debt, display a gentle "Wealth Tip" (e.g., "Paying off your 20% VISA card is a guaranteed 20% return. Consider doing that first."), but allow them to proceed.
   * Output: "You have $2,000/month surplus. We recommend setting up an automated transfer of $1,000 (biweekly $500) to your brokerage."
Phase 3: The Psychologist (Risk Assessment)
* User Action: Slider for Age. Multiple-choice for "Market Crash Reaction."
* System Logic: Translates "feelings" into a mathematical Risk Score (0.0 to 1.0).
Phase 4: Dual Outputs (The Deliverable)
The app generates two distinct plans side-by-side.
Output A: The Lump Sum Deployment (The "Clean Up")
* Purpose: How to invest the $50k sitting in savings right now.
* Visual: Pie Chart & Table.
* Columns: Ticker | Name | Allocation % | $ Amount | Why It's Here
* Logic: Often leans slightly more conservative to prevent "Shock" from dumping a large sum into the market at once.
Output B: The Biweekly Engine (The "Build Up")
* Purpose: The automated buy order for the $500 paycheck deduction.
* Visual: Table.
* Columns: Ticker | Allocation % | $ Buy Amount
* Logic: Aggressive Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA).

Part 3: Gemini Model Landscape (Dec 2025 Status)
Objective: Choosing the right "Brain" for your application.
1. Gemini 2.5 Flash (The Default)
* Status: Available Now.
* Role: The engine for this app.
* Why: It is fast enough to make the "Audit" feel instant. It is cheap enough that you won't pay anything during testing.
* Cost: ~$0.10 / 1M tokens (Free tier available).
2. Gemini 3 Pro (The Auditor)
* Status: Available (Preview).
* Role: Optional "Deep Audit."
* Why: If a user pastes a very complex list of assets (e.g., "I have 500 shares of TSLA bought at $200, options on AMD..."), Gemini 3 Pro's reasoning capabilities are superior for giving nuanced advice on how to unwind that position.
* Cost: ~$2.00 / 1M tokens (Significantly higher).
3. Gemini 3 Flash (The Future)
* Status: NOT RELEASED YET.
* Strategy: We build with 2.5 Flash. When 3.0 Flash drops, you simply change one line of code in backend.py: MODEL_NAME = "gemini-3.0-flash"
