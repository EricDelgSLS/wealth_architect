WEALTH ARCHITECT - TEST SCENARIOS
==================================
Date: January 17, 2026
Purpose: Comprehensive test cases to validate portfolio diversity and prompt quality

These scenarios test different combinations of age, risk tolerance, convictions, and financial situations
to ensure the AI produces meaningfully different portfolios.

==================================
TESTING CHECKLIST
==================================

For each scenario, verify:

□ Portfolio allocations sum to exactly 100%
□ No single stock exceeds 5%
□ No single sector exceeds 15%
□ At least 60% in diversified broad-market ETFs
□ Equity/bond split matches risk profile (A=40/60, B=60/40, C=80/20, D=90/10)
□ User convictions are explicitly addressed in "Why This Portfolio Works For You"
□ Age and timeline are referenced in strategy
□ Tables are formatted correctly (Lump Sum, Biweekly DCA, Holdings Explained)
□ Plain language explanations (no jargon)
□ Expert frameworks are evident in choices (but not explicitly mentioned)

**Diversity Test:**
Compare Scenario 1, 4, and 9 side-by-side. Portfolios should be MEANINGFULLY different in:
- Equity/bond allocation
- Sector tilts
- Risk level
- Explanations

If portfolios are too similar, prompt needs refinement.
